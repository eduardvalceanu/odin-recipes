Pork Barbecue

You need only five ingredients to fix this sweet and tender pork for sandwiches. Feel free to adjust the sauce ingredients to suit your family's tastes.

5 hours
10 servings

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Cut roast in half.
  2. Place in a 5-qt. slow cooker.
  3. Sprinkle with seasoned salt and garlic powder.
  4. Pour 1/4 cup cola over roast.
  5. Cover and cook on low for 4-5 hours or until meat is tender.
  6. Remove roast; cool slightly.
  7. Shred meat with 2 forks and return to slow cooker.
  8. Combine barbecue sauce and remaining cola; pour over meat.
  9. Cover and cook on high for 1-2 hours or until sauce is thickened.
  10. Serve on buns.
